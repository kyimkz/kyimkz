About Us

Kyim - is a fashion brand, a team of professionals and a huge community of girls who are in love with fashion.


Design studio - Kyim is located in the heart of Asia - Kazakhstan. The main idea of the brand is to offer dozens of new designs of clothing and accessories every week!


The Kyim girls love high fashion! She loves how we transform trends and create clothes that can be worn every day.

 

People follow us on social networks to see the latest in the fashion industry and what to wear them with, because we always demonstrate several combination options.


We try to simplify the technological cycle of clothing production so that the time from the creation of a design to its availability for sale takes no more than a couple of weeks. That's why Kyim's images are always fresh.


Our company does not use minor labor and places orders only in large professional factories with the best working conditions.


Every day, thousands of girls unpack packages with new Kyim products and become happier, because it is obvious that a new dress can change the day, and with it, their whole life!


We love fashion, we change and grow every day.


Follow us and you will see a lot of interesting things!
